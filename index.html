About the foundation

Serving Uganda's poorest communities with dignity, unity, and faith.

Al-Ihsan Charity Foundation is a charity organization registered in 2022 with offices on Accacia Avenue, Jinja City Central Division, Jinja City, Republic of Uganda.

The foundation works to improve the social well-being of elders, widows, orphans, teenage mothers, and vulnerable families regardless of race or religion.

Vision

To improve the social well-being of elders, widows, orphans, and teenage mothers in the poorest communities of Uganda.

Mission

To create unity and development in the poorest communities for self-sustainability.

From the project coordinator

Every gift should arrive with trust, dignity, and visible proof.

At Al-Ihsan, we want donors to see more than a promise. We want them to see the well completed, the Quran delivered, the family reached, and the next need clearly before them.

Masjid construction

Build and renovate masjids

Support remote villages where people cannot access places of worship. One completed masjid cost about $20,000 from foundation to painting.

"Whoever built a mosque... Allah would build for him a similar place in Paradise." Sahih al-Bukhari 450

$20,000complete masjid target
Water wells

Construct long lasting water wells

Provide clean water wells dug about 35 to 45 feet deep using machines and manpower for communities still in urgent need.

Water wells are sadaqah jariyah: a continuing charity whose reward keeps reaching. Sahih Muslim 1631

$1,100per water well
Food packs

Feed vulnerable families

Uganda has families surviving on one meal a day. Food packs help less privileged households and daily orphan feeding.

"Charity does not decrease wealth." Sahih Muslim 2588

$30food pack, $1 orphan meal
Ramadhan iftar

Share iftar with fasting families

Support Ramadhan meals and food pack distributions for families, children, elders, and communities in need.

A fast can end with relief, dignity, and dua when a family receives food on time. Ramadhan relief

$3iftar meal, $10 pack
Qurban

Distribute fresh meat

Provide qurban meat to the poorest families who cannot afford fresh meat in their homes.
